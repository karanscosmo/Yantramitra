# Thermax 30 Ton Package Boiler Operation & Safety Manual

**Document ID:** MAN-UTL-BLR30T-2026  
**OEM Vendor:** Thermax Industrial Steam Systems  
**Applicable Facility:** Ahmedabad Utility Block  

## 1. Pressure Vessel Rating & Fuel System
- **MAWP:** 17.5 bar (253 psi).
- **Steam Output:** 30,000 kg/hr dry saturated steam at 206°C.
- **Burner Fuel:** Natural Gas / Light Diesel Oil (LDO) dual-fuel modulating burner.

## 2. Safety Interlocks & Blowdown Protocols
- **Low-Low Water Level Trip:** Conduct daily float switch test on Level Controller LC-101.
- **FD Fan Pressure Switch:** Set to 15 mbar static pressure minimum.
- **Continuous Blowdown Valve (CBD):** Adjust CBD valve to maintain boiler water TDS < 2,200 ppm.
