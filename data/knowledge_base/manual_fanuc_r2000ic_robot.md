# Fanuc R-2000iC 6-Axis Robot Overhaul & Servo Guide

**Document ID:** MAN-ROB-R2000-2026  
**OEM Vendor:** Fanuc Robotics Corp.  

## 1. Servo Motor Axis Lubrication
- **Reduction Gear Grease:** Kyodo Yushi Vigo Grease RE0.
- **Grease Replacement Interval:** 3,000 operating hours or 1 year.
- **J1/J2 Axis Drain Pressure:** Do not exceed 0.2 bar during grease flushing to prevent seal blowout.
